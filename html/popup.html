The extension will prioritize your selected quality if available. Otherwise, it will select the nearest higher quality. In cases where higher qualities are not available, it will choose the highest available quality.

This extension will continuously monitor video links and as soon as a new video is opened, it will select your preferred quality setting or the highest available quality if your preferred option is not available. If the user manually changes the video quality, the extension will respect that choice for the duration set above. After the configured time elapses, the extension will step in to re-select your preferred quality for the same video.
